Chiesa Nuova/Bar Internazionale to Hostel Brikette

Micro‑route from the Chiesa Nuova stop to the hostel with minimal stairs; luggage tips and alternatives.

Arriving by bus? “Chiesa Nuova” or “Bar Internazionale” is the closest stop to the hostel. This micro-route minimises stairs for knees and luggage.

Why this stop works

The bus stop outside the cafe/bar called Bar Internazionale is roughly 140 metres (490 feet) from Hostel Brikette.

Bus timing & tickets

Buy SITA tickets at the tabacchi near the stop or validate pre-bought tickets on boarding; keep a spare for late-night returns.

Evening buses thin out after 22:00—check the timetable in advance and aim for the second-to-last service to avoid taxi prices.

Before you head out

  • Check SITA schedules in advance so you are not waiting on the roadside for the last service.
  • Validate your ticket as soon as you board—inspectors fine unvalidated tickets even for short rides.
  • Message the hostel if you are arriving after midnight so overnight staff can keep the door code handy.

Step‑by‑step

  1. From the stop outside Bar Internazionale, walk just over 100 metres (330 feet) uphill along Viale Pasitea — follow the walking route in the map below for turn-by-turn guidance.
  2. At the bend, continue past the terrace-level Hostel Brikette sign, then take the stairs rising on the right-hand side of the road up to the main door.

Legs & luggage

  • The entire walk is paved with only one short staircase at the entrance.
  • If slopes bother your knees, take short breaks—traffic slows naturally on the bend.
  • Arriving via ferry first? Dock → Hostel luggage route.
  • Need a hand with heavy bags? Porter service guide.

FAQs

Is there a sidewalk the whole way?

Yes — the route uses the main road pavement, avoiding long staircases.

Is it lit at night?

Street lighting is present; use your phone torch for extra visibility on alleys.
