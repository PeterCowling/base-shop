# Deep Research Prompt - MARKET-01 Competitor mapping

Objective: produce a decision-grade competitor map for `<BIZ>`.

Required output sections:
1. Direct competitor set (top 5-10) with evidence links.
2. Indirect/substitute options and why they compete.
3. Feature and positioning comparison matrix.
4. Observable strengths/weaknesses by competitor.
5. Open questions and confidence tags.

Rules:
- Use evidence-backed claims only.
- Mark unknowns explicitly.
- Separate observed facts from assumptions.
