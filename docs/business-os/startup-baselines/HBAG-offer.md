# HBAG Offer Design (S2B Backfill)

**Executive summary:** HBAG is a premium Birkin-style mini handbag accessories brand targeting
fashion-conscious women who follow the bag charm trend and want artisan-quality pieces at
€80–€150 — the white space between Etsy mid-market (€15–€60) and accessible luxury (Coach $95,
Miu Miu €200+). The 90-day offer stack is built around two validated demand-test candidates:
H1 (bag charm) and H2 (AirPod holder), both using the same physical product body with
different positioning and hardware. The H3 in-destination Amalfi variant adds a location
story that is uniquely defensible given the operator's presence in Positano. Photography
and supply chain confirmation are the immediate gates before any channel launch.

---

## Section 1: ICP Segmentation

### ICP-A: The Bag Charm Buyer (Primary — H1 launch ICP)

**Demographics:**
- Age: 25–40
- Geography: International (EU and North American primary); Amalfi Coast tourists in season
- Income: comfortable spending €80–€150 on a considered fashion accessory purchase
- Device: mobile-first; discovers via Instagram Reels, TikTok, Pinterest
- She owns at least one aspirational handbag (not necessarily designer, but invested-in)

**Psychographics:**
- Follows the bag charm trend; has seen it on her feed multiple times; is actively looking for the right piece
- Values artisan quality and brand story over logo; "quiet fashion" sensibility
- The €80–€150 price point feels justified for something she'll wear every day on her bag
- Discovers via visual channels (Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest) and purchases via Etsy or direct DM
- She doesn't want something generic; the Birkin-style reference is aspirational but accessible

**Jobs-to-be-done:**
- Functional: add a considered accessory to her handbag that makes it feel more personal and curated
- Emotional: feel like she has a "complete" bag — the whole look pulled together
- Social: earn unprompted comments from people who notice and ask "where did you get that?"

**Buying triggers:**
- Sees the bag charm trend on her social feed and wants in before it feels overdone
- Gets a new handbag and wants to build a look around it
- Gifting occasion — treat herself or gift for a friend

### ICP-B: The AirPod Case Shopper (Secondary — H2 launch ICP)

**Demographics:**
- Age: 22–38
- Geography: Global (Etsy reaches this buyer wherever Apple ecosystem users shop)
- Income: spends on tech accessories; Apple ecosystem user; views AirPods as a daily-carry item worth accessorising

**Psychographics:**
- Wants her AirPod case to look as good as her other accessories — not clinical or generic white plastic
- The "fashion AirPod case" category is established at ultra-luxury (Hermes $930); she wants the artisan version at a real-world price
- Discovers via Etsy search ("leather AirPod case", "luxury AirPod holder") or via fashion content creators

**Jobs-to-be-done:**
- Functional: carry AirPods in something that looks good clipped to a bag, belt, or in a pocket
- Emotional: feel like her accessories are coordinated and considered — the same reason she has a leather card holder instead of a cheap plastic one
- Social: show-and-tell when friends notice it

**Buying triggers:**
- Gets new AirPods Pro or AirPods 4 and wants a case that matches her style
- Her existing case is scratched / doesn't feel premium enough
- Gift for a friend who has "everything" but hasn't found a nice AirPod case

### ICP-C: The Amalfi Tourist (Secondary — H3 seasonal ICP)

**Demographics:**
- Age: 30–55
- Geography: International tourist visiting Amalfi Coast June–September
- Spend: €391–$1,098/day luxury daily spend profile; fashion/accessories = 12.2% of Italy tourist spend
- Context: premium souvenir category — wants something authentic, not a mass-market item

**Psychographics:**
- Buys artisan goods in-destination as part of the travel experience
- The leather sandal (€100–€250) is the established Positano premium souvenir precedent — HBAG is the handbag equivalent
- "I bought this in Positano" is the purchase rationale; location story is the core value

**Jobs-to-be-done:**
- Functional: acquire a quality, location-specific souvenir that she'll actually use
- Emotional: take home a piece of the destination — something that tells a story
- Social: show friends "this is what I found in Positano" — the provenance is the point

**Buying triggers:**
- Walking through Positano / visiting Brikette hostel and sees the product on display
- Specifically looking for artisan fashion accessories as a souvenir
- Holiday gift shopping mode

## Section 3: Offer Structure

### Core Offer (90-day demand test scope)

**H1 — Mini Bag Charm (primary launch product)**
- The Birkin-style mini bag worn as a charm on a full-size handbag strap via clip/keyring
- Materials: leather-look exterior (same body as all variants), gold hardware, magnetic or zipper closure
- Attachment: standard carabiner or keyring clip to any D-ring or bag strap loop
- Price: €80–€150 (Premium tier; exact price TBD pending TASK-02 margin stack)
- Channel: Etsy listing + Instagram/TikTok demand probe

**H2 — AirPod / Small Items Holder (secondary launch product)**
- Same physical body; sized to hold AirPods Pro or AirPods 4 case + a few cards
- Clip attachment to bag strap
- Price: €80–€150 (same premium tier)
- Confirmed: AirPods Pro and AirPods 4 fit verified (TASK-03, 2026-02-17)
- Channel: Etsy listing

**H3 — Amalfi Location Variant (seasonal, post-checkpoint)**
- Same body with Amalfi Coast / Positano imagery applied
- In-destination only: Brikette hostel reception + 1 Positano boutique consignment
- Tourist season: June–September
- Price: €100–€150 (can command location premium)
- Deferred to TASK-10 post-checkpoint

### Bundles / Variants at Launch

No bundles at demand test phase — single-SKU approach to get clean signal on each variant individually.

Post-checkpoint candidate bundles (not in scope for demand test):
- Bag charm + AirPod holder set (complementary carry pair)
- Amalfi gift set: H3 location variant + location-branded gift pouch

### Included Deliverables

- The mini bag itself (with attachment hardware)
- Tissue + ribbon packaging (giftable out of the box — the Etsy emotional differentiator)
- Handwritten note from maker (Pete/HBAG: the artisan authenticity signal that Etsy premium buyers respond to strongly)
- Care card (leather-look material care instructions)

### Exclusions (at launch)

- No personalisation at launch (name engraving, custom colour — deferred to post-10 sales)
- No wholesale B2B outreach (deferred to post-50 DTC sales)
- No app or own-site (pre-website mode; Etsy + in-destination only)
- No H4 (dog holder) or H5 (first bag) until post-checkpoint

### Guarantees / Risk Reversals

- **30-day free exchange** on any order (wrong colour, changed mind, doesn't feel right — one exchange per order, no fault required)
- **90-day hardware guarantee**: if the clip or closure hardware fails within 90 days of normal use, replacement at no cost
- Clear sizing and compatibility information in listings (AirPod model compatibility explicitly stated for H2)

## Section 4: Positioning One-Pager

### Positioning Statement (Moore Template)

```
For fashion-conscious women 25–40 who love their handbags and follow the bag charm trend,
who want a premium artisan piece that makes their bag feel complete — at a real-world price,
[HBAG Brand Name] is a premium mini handbag accessories brand,
that delivers Birkin-style craftsmanship as a wearable bag charm, AirPod holder, or Amalfi keepsake at €80–€150 —
unlike generic Etsy sellers (no brand story, low perceived quality) or Miu Miu / Coach (€200+, not accessible),
because HBAG is made by an artisan in Italy with a real location story and a 30-day free exchange guarantee.
```

### Category Frame

- **Primary category**: artisan fashion accessories — bag charms and mini bag accessories
- **Not positioned as**: generic Etsy mini bag, tech accessory brand, children's toy accessory
- The bag charm trend is the entry point, not the identity — HBAG will outlast the trend by becoming a premium accessories name with Italian origin story

### Competitive Frame

Customers compare HBAG against:
1. **Etsy artisan sellers** (€15–€60 range) — primary comparison at launch; HBAG differentiates on brand coherence, quality story, photography, and artisan packaging
2. **Coach bag charm** ($95) — accessible-luxury anchor; HBAG can be at, above, or below this based on photography perception test result
3. **Miu Miu/Loewe** (€200–€450) — aspirational ceiling; HBAG is "Miu Miu look at a craft price"
4. **Hermes/Chanel AirPod cases** ($930/$950) — for H2 specifically: "what you want without the Hermes price tag"

### Key Message

*The bag, complete.* (Primary — bag charm positioning)

*Your AirPods, finally dressed right.* (Secondary — H2 AirPod holder positioning)

*Made in Positano. The one thing worth bringing home.* (Tertiary — H3 in-destination)

## Section 5: Pricing / Packaging Hypothesis

**THIS IS A HYPOTHESIS TO VALIDATE. Final prices depend on TASK-02 (supply chain cost).**

### Proposed Price Points

| Variant | Price hypothesis | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| H1 bag charm | **€95–€120** | Above Coach charm ($95 anchor); below Miu Miu entry (€200); Etsy premium tier ceiling is ~€60–80; €95 opens the artisan-brand tier |
| H2 AirPod holder | **€80–€100** | KILLSPENCER $55 sets artisan floor; Hermes $930 sets ceiling; €80–€100 is the accessible artisan white space with clear "not a luxury brand" signal |
| H3 Amalfi location | **€100–€150** | Location premium justified; tourist context means less price sensitivity; leather sandal €100–€250 anchors the expectation |

### Pricing Model

One-time purchase. No subscription. No tiers at launch.

### Competitor Price Comparison

| Brand / Product | Price | Tier | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Generic Etsy mini bag sellers | €15–€40 | Mid-market / no brand | Volume play; no craft story; HBAG is not competing here |
| KILLSPENCER (artisan AirPod holder) | $55 | Artisan mid-market | Function-forward; not fashion-first; the artisan floor |
| Coach bag charm | $95 | Accessible luxury | Mass-market but premium anchor; HBAG targets at/above |
| Hermes AirPod case | $930 | Ultra-luxury | Proves buyer exists; HBAG is the "artisan alternative" at 10× lower price |
| Chanel AirPod case | $950 | Ultra-luxury | Same: ceiling validation |
| Miu Miu mini bag charm | €200–€300 | Accessible luxury | Aspirational ceiling for ICP-A; HBAG sits below this intentionally |
| Loewe / Balenciaga bag charms | €450–€1,990 | Ultra-luxury | Top of market; sets category aspiration |
| Pagerie "The Pochette" | $152 | Luxury artisan (dog) | H4 ceiling reference (dog holder variant) |
| Leather sandal (Positano) | €100–€250 | Artisan premium souvenir | In-destination H3 price anchor |
| **HBAG H1 (bag charm)** | **€95–€120** | **Artisan premium** | **Target: above Coach, below Miu Miu** |
| **HBAG H2 (AirPod holder)** | **€80–€100** | **Artisan premium** | **Target: above KILLSPENCER, below luxury threshold** |
| **HBAG H3 (Amalfi)** | **€100–€150** | **Premium souvenir** | **Competes with leather sandal precedent** |

### Pricing Rationale

1. **Competitive gap confirmed**: No brand coherently owns €80–€150 artisan premium mini bag accessories in the EU market at scale on Etsy.
2. **Bag charm trend creates first-mover window**: At 12× wholesale growth, this is the moment to establish a premium price point before the category becomes crowded.
3. **Photography perception gates pricing**: Prices above €80 require product photos that read as premium. TASK-06 perception test (≥3/5 estimate ≥€80 on first contact) is the constraint.
4. **Supply chain cost unknown**: TASK-02 will determine the price floor. If contribution margin at €80 is <35%, price must adjust upward before Etsy launch.

### Confidence Level

**MEDIUM.** Market signals strongly support demand at €80–€150 from multiple independent sources. Two critical risks remain: (1) product quality reads as premium in photos (unverified — TASK-06 gate); (2) supply chain cost may compress margin at lower end of range (unverified — TASK-02 gate). Confidence moves to HIGH when: 3 paid sales at target price confirmed, no price-shock objections in DMs.

### Validation Plan

1. TASK-06: Photography perception test — ≥3/5 cold reviewers estimate ≥€80 for H1 lead photo
2. TASK-07/08: Open Etsy listing at €95–€120 (H1) and €80–€100 (H2); run Instagram probe for 28 days
3. Track: Are DM inquirers asking "is this the price?" (price shock) or "how do I buy?" (acceptance)?
4. Checkpoint (TASK-09): If 0 sales and price-shock DMs, test 10% lower for 2 weeks before concluding demand is absent
