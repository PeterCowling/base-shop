Hostel Brikette Travel Guide

Naples Center to Positano – Train then Bus

Follow these directions to travel from central Naples to Hostel Brikette via the Circumvesuviana train to Sorrento and the SITA bus to Positano. The guide includes ticket tips, bus timetable advice, and photos of your final approach to the hostel.

Tip

Need other routes? Explore the full set of arrival directions on the How to Get Here page.

Train then Bus overview

This journey covers the fastest public transport combination from Naples Centre: the Circumvesuviana train to Sorrento followed by the SITA bus to Positano. It does not cover travel from Naples Airport.

Naples train station

Circumvesuviana services to Sorrento depart from the lower level of Napoli Garibaldi station (beneath Napoli Centrale). Buy your ticket before heading down to the platforms, verify which track your service uses, and board the train towards Sorrento.

Sorrento train station

After arriving in Sorrento, exit the station and purchase your SITA bus ticket from the nearby bar, newsstand, or tobacco shop — tickets are not sold on the bus. The SITA stop sits at the bottom of the stairs outside the station; wait for the Amalfi-bound bus and ride it to Positano.

Reading the SITA timetable

The SITA bus schedule can be confusing — use these quick pointers to locate the correct service:

Select the departure row for Sorrento to find your bus time. Check the Positano (Chiesa Nuova) row in the same column to see when you will arrive.

View the latest timetable on positano.com.

positano.com

  • The rows in the first column represent bus stops along the route.
  • Each column is a scheduled journey labelled with a letter.
  • For travel Monday to Saturday, use columns 'F' and 'G'.
  • For Sunday or public holidays, use columns 'G' and 'H'.

Positano bus stop

  • The closest stop is Positano (Chiesa Nuova), approximately 140 metres (490 feet) from the hostel.
  • It sits outside the cafe/bar Bar Internazionale.
  • The bus drops you at road level — no long staircases are required to reach us.

Walk from the bus stop

Street view showing the Hostel Brikette sign above Via G. Marconi

Via G. Marconi — walk past the sign, then take the stairs on the right.

  • Walk just over 100 metres / 330 feet from the bus stop.
  • Turn right and follow the steps up to the hostel's main door.

On arrival

Look for our sign on the second-floor terrace. Keep walking past it, follow the bend, and climb the stairs on the right-hand side of the road to reach the main entrance.
