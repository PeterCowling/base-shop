Hostel Brikette Travel Guide

Positano to Ravello — Ferry then Bus (via Amalfi)

Use this guide to travel from Positano to Ravello by combining a scenic ferry to Amalfi and a SITA bus up to Ravello. It covers the walk to the port, ticket tips, where to transfer in Amalfi, and what to expect on arrival in Ravello.

Tip

Need other transport options? Open the How to Get Here overview for buses, airport transfers, and mixed itineraries.

Journey overview

  • Route: Positano → Amalfi by ferry, then Amalfi → Ravello by SITA bus.
  • Allow extra time in high season for queues and connections.
  • Check live ferry and bus schedules on the day of travel.

Walk to Positano’s port

You can follow a well-trodden stair route from the hostel down to Spiaggia Grande. If you have heavy luggage, consider sending bags with the porter service and walking light.

Prefer a map-based walkthrough? See Chiesa Nuova → Beach (fewest stairs).

  • Exit Hostel Brikette, go down the steps to the main road, and turn left (Via G. Marconi).
  • Walk a little over 100 m to the right-hand turn (tabacchi on the corner).
  • Buy a bus ticket for Amalfi → Ravello at the tabacchi if convenient.
  • Continue downhill; at the first hairpin bend, take the stairs shortcut off the road.
  • Follow the stairs and paths downhill, crossing the road where indicated.
  • At the bottom, turn right, pass through the archway, and continue ~50 m to the port.

Buy ferry tickets

Check latest times and prices on Travelmar and Positano.com – Ferry schedules.

  • At the port, compare departures and prices at the operator booths.
  • Typical operators: Travelmar and Lucibello (confirm next departure to Amalfi).
  • Arrive 15–20 minutes early in summer to board calmly.

Transfer in Amalfi

Consult SITA routes and times on Positano.com (SITA timetable overview).

  • Disembark at Amalfi and walk a short distance to the bus stop area.
  • If you didn’t buy a ticket in Positano, purchase an Amalfi → Ravello ticket now.
  • Queue for the Ravello-bound bus; routes are typically 5110/5120 (check the display).

Arriving in Ravello

  • Ravello sits above Amalfi; many sights cluster around Piazza Duomo.
  • Follow local signage to your accommodation from the drop-off point.
  • For late arrivals or long gaps, consider a taxi from Amalfi instead of waiting.
