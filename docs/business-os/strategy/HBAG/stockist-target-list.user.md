## Evaluation Rules

- Prefer fashion-first merchandising over generic souvenir retail.
- Prefer doors with tourist spend and taste over raw footfall.
- Prefer staff who will explain or style the item instead of leaving it uncontextualized.
- Prefer strong visual environments that can also create social/content spillover.
- Prefer dedicated placement over buried display.
- Accept consignment only when the door is strategically valuable and display quality is controlled.

## Do Not Do Now

- No broad souvenir wholesale.
- No discount-led sell-in.
- No dog-first hero messaging in boutique conversations.
- No large SKU expansion to satisfy retailer curiosity before the core fashion signal is proven.
