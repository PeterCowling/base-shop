# <BIZ> Market Intelligence Pack

## 1) Executive Summary

- 8-12 bullets: the most important commercial truths for this business right now.

## 2) Business Context (Input Packet)

- Business idea
- Product list and specs
- Target customer (initial ICP)
- Planned channels
- Budget and stock constraints
- Launch-surface mode (`website-live` or `pre-website`)

## 3) Market Size and Demand Signals

- TAM/SAM/SOM ranges (with clear assumptions)
- Search/social/marketplace demand signals
- Seasonality and timing effects
- Evidence quality tags (`observed` vs `inferred`)

## 4) Competitor and Substitute Landscape

- Direct competitors table
- Adjacent/substitute solutions table
- Price bands and offer structures
- Positioning patterns and gaps

## 5) Customer Segments and JTBD

- Primary ICP and why now
- Secondary ICPs and activation sequence
- Jobs-to-be-done, pains, and buying triggers
- Objections and trust barriers

## 6) Unit Economics Priors

- Expected AOV range
- CAC/CPC priors by channel
- Return-rate priors
- Gross-margin priors
- Guardrails that would force stop/pivot

## 7) Channel Strategy Implications

- Channel priority (first 90 days)
- Channel-specific hypotheses
- Minimum measurement instrumentation needed

## 8) Website Design Implications

- Information architecture requirements
- PDP content/UX requirements
- Trust/compliance requirements
- Checkout/payment requirements
- Support and post-purchase requirements

## 9) Product Design Implications

- Must-have product requirements
- Nice-to-have features
- Compatibility/fit requirements
- Quality/risk hotspots and tests
- Packaging/unboxing implications

## 10) Regulatory and Claims Constraints

- Non-negotiable legal/compliance constraints
- Claim boundaries (what cannot be said)
- Returns/withdrawal obligations

## 11) 90-Day Outcome Proposals

- Proposed outcome statement
- Baseline, target, by-date, owner
- Leading indicators
- Decision links

## 12) First-14-Day Validation Plan

- High-impact tests
- Pass/fail thresholds
- Re-forecast triggers

## 13) Assumptions and Risks

- Assumptions register with confidence and impact
- Top risks and mitigations

## 14) Source List

- URL + access date
- Short note on reliability/relevance

## 15) Freshness Contract

- `Last-reviewed` cadence: max 30 days for startup businesses.
- Trigger immediate refresh when: major competitor move, pricing shift, channel change, or product change.
