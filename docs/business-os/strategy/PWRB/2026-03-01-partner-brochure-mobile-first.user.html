Common questions

Are these final terms?
No. This document is for interest-stage discussion. Final terms are agreed only after fit-check and pilot review.
Will my staff have to manage the rentals?
No. Guests complete the entire rental — payment, unit release, and return — through a QR code flow on their own phone. Staff only need to point to the station if someone asks.
What happens if the station runs empty or has a fault?
Our operations team monitors station levels and handles refill, swap, and fault response. You do not need to report or manage this — your rep will confirm the SLA for your venue.
What if a guest doesn't return the unit?
A €25 deposit hold is placed on the guest's card at rental. If the unit is not returned within 48 hours, the hold converts to a purchase charge. Your venue commission is unaffected by non-returns.
