# Deep Research Prompt - MARKET-02 Demand evidence

Objective: produce demand evidence for `<BIZ>` and target segments.

Required output sections:
1. Demand indicators (search, social, marketplace, forum, review signals).
2. Segment-level pull signals and urgency cues.
3. Seasonality and timing effects.
4. Counter-signals (weak demand or saturation warnings).
5. Confidence scoring and evidence log.

Rules:
- Cite sources for every non-trivial claim.
- Distinguish fact vs inferred interpretation.
- Include unresolved unknowns.
