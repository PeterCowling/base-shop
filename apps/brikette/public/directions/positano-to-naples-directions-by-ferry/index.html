Getting to Positano’s Port

We have very detailed directions for walking down to Positano’s port.  

If you plan on walking down to Positano's port, we recommend giving your bags to the porters to make the journey easier.  To ensure that your bags are ready for your ferry departure, arrange for the porters to collect them about 1.5 to 2 hours before your scheduled departure time. For more information on the porter service, please refer to our help system.
