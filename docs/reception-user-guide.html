How the App Is Organised

Admin

6 screens
🔑

Security & Login

You sign in with your email and password. For quick access, you can also set up a 6-digit PIN to unlock the app. If you step away from the screen for 60 seconds, the app automatically locks itself to keep things secure.

🌙

Dark Mode

You can switch between a light and dark colour scheme at any time from the login screen. Your preference is saved so the app always opens the way you like it.

Area 1

Operations

The daily essentials — everything you need to serve guests, manage the bar, and handle check-ins and checkouts.

All Staff

Bar & Café

The main point-of-sale screen for serving drinks and food. Browse products by category (alcoholic, non-alcoholic, food, other), take orders, and process payments by cash or card. Confirmed orders appear as tickets on the sales board so the bar and kitchen know what to prepare.

Order Taking Cash & Card Kitchen Display Breakfast Plans
All Staff

Check-In

See today's arriving guests in a clear table. Create new bookings, add guests, scan or enter passport and ID details, and mark arrivals as checked in. Each guest row links to the document capture form for recording identity information.

Arrivals List ID Capture New Bookings
All Staff

View Rooms

A visual calendar grid showing which rooms are booked, free, or awaiting arrival over the next two weeks. Each room displays colour-coded bars so you can see at a glance what's happening across the whole property.

Room Grid 2-Week View Colour-Coded
All Staff

Checkout

Lists all guests due to leave today. Check that keycards and towels have been returned, confirm bag storage arrangements, process any deposit refunds, and mark each guest as checked out.

Departures List Loan Returns Deposit Refunds
All Staff

Loans

Track items loaned to guests — keycards, towels, adaptors, and anything else. Record when items go out and when they come back. If a deposit was taken, the system tracks the amount so you can refund it on return.

Keycard Tracking Deposit Ledger Return Logging
All Staff

Stay Extension

When a guest wants to stay longer, this screen shows every in-house guest with their room and nightly rate. It checks room availability automatically, calculates city tax, and lets you process the payment for the extra nights.

Availability Check City Tax Payment
All Staff

Guest Requests

Guests can submit requests through the guest app (Prime) — things like extending their stay, storing luggage, or changing a meal plan. These requests appear here as a queue for staff to approve, decline, or mark as completed.

Request Queue Approve / Decline Bag Storage
Area 2

Till & Cash

Everything to do with money — opening and closing shifts, counting cash, reconciling with the bank, and spotting discrepancies.

🔒

Owner Access Only

The Till & Cash area is restricted to the owner account. Staff handle payments through the Bar screen; the till management screens are used by the owner to oversee cash handling.

Owner

Till

Open and close cash register shifts. At the start, enter your opening cash count. At the end, count the drawer and the system compares what you have against what it expected. Track credit card slips, keycard deposits, and any cash exchanges during the shift.

Open / Close Shift Cash Count Variance Check
Owner

Safe

Manage the property's safe. Count the physical cash (broken down by denomination) and compare it to the expected balance. Record bank deposits and petty cash withdrawals. Everything is tracked so the numbers always add up.

Safe Count Bank Deposits Petty Cash
Owner

Workbench

Cross-check what the property management system says was charged (cash and card) against what actually came through the card terminal. Highlights any discrepancies between the two so nothing slips through the cracks.

PMS vs Terminal Discrepancy Alerts
Owner

Live Shift View

A real-time dashboard of the current open shift. See every transaction as it happens — opening cash, credit slips, card totals, keycard loans and returns. Useful for keeping an eye on things without waiting for the shift to close.

Real-Time All Transactions
Owner

Variance Heatmap

A colour-coded grid showing how accurate each staff member's cash handling is across their shifts. Green means the count was spot on, yellow means a small difference, and red flags a larger discrepancy worth investigating.

Per-Staff View Colour-Coded Thresholds
Owner

End of Day Report

Generates a printable summary of the entire day: total sales, credit slip entries, safe balance, keycard movements, variances, and any irregularities. Print it for your records or review it on screen.

Daily Summary Printable Full Audit
Area 3

Management

Housekeeping priorities, pre-arrival payments, email tracking, and a powerful search tool to find any booking or transaction.

All Staff

Room Preparation

Shows housekeeping which rooms need cleaning and in what order. The dashboard displays occupancy, checkout counts, and a priority ranking so cleaners tackle the most urgent rooms first.

Cleaning Priority Room Status Date Picker
All Staff

Prepayments

Manage credit card payments taken before guests arrive. See three queues: guests who agreed to terms and are awaiting a first charge, those whose first attempt failed, and those whose second attempt failed. Process payments, update card details, or mark bookings as paid.

Payment Queues Card Management Status Tracking
All Staff

Email Tracking

Track which automated emails have been sent to guests — booking confirmations, pre-arrival reminders, and follow-ups. Search by booking reference or guest name, and log the next step in the email sequence.

Email Progress Activity Logging Search
All Staff

Search & Audit

A powerful search tool with three tabs. Bookings: find any booking by name, reference, date, room, or status. Transactions: look up financial records. Audits: review the audit trail for any transaction. Each booking shows its full lifecycle from creation through checkout.

Booking Search Transaction Lookup Audit Trail
Area 4

Admin

Compliance reporting, stock control, and performance dashboards to help you make better decisions.

🔒

Owner Access Only

The Admin area is restricted to the owner account. These screens handle regulatory compliance, inventory management, and business analytics.

Owner

Police Registration

Italian law requires that guest check-ins are reported to the Alloggiati police system. This screen shows yesterday's arrivals, lets you select which guests to submit, and sends the records electronically. Colour-coded rows show which submissions succeeded and which need attention.

Alloggiati Legal Compliance Status Tracking
Owner

Bar Stock

Keep track of bar and beverage inventory. See expected stock counts for every product, log new purchases, record re-counts, and note any leakage or wastage. Covers all bar categories from coffee to cocktails.

Stock Levels Purchase Logging Wastage Tracking
Owner

Kitchen Ingredients

Track kitchen ingredient quantities separately from bar stock. Update counts as ingredients are used or purchased. Maintains an audit trail so you can see exactly when and how stock levels changed.

Ingredient Counts Audit Trail
Owner

Real-Time Dashboard

Live charts that update every 60 seconds. See daily sales totals over time, how payments split between cash and card, and a history of cash variances from recent shift closings. A quick snapshot of how the business is performing right now.

Live Charts Sales Trends Payment Breakdown
Owner

Menu Performance

Understand which menu items make the most money. Charts show revenue and estimated profit margin by category (coffee, beer, cocktails, food), peak selling hours, and which items drive the most add-on sales.

Revenue by Category Margin Analysis Peak Hours
Owner

Connection Test

A simple diagnostic tool to check that the app can reach its backend services. Tap the button — if the connection is healthy, you're good to go.

Connectivity Diagnostics
Workflow

A Typical Day

Here's how everything fits together during a normal working day.

1

Open Shift

Count opening cash and start a new till shift

2

Morning Prep

Check the room preparation dashboard for cleaning priorities

3

Checkouts

Process departures, collect keycards, refund deposits

4

Bar Service

Take orders, serve food and drinks throughout the day

5

Check-ins

Welcome arriving guests, capture IDs, issue keycards

6

Close Shift

Count cash, reconcile the till, review the end-of-day report

📱

Works Offline Too

Reception is a progressive web app. If your internet connection drops briefly, you can keep working and everything syncs back up when you're online again. A small indicator appears at the top of the screen if you're offline.

👥

Guest Requests via the Guest App

Guests can use the separate "Prime" app on their phone to request things like a stay extension, luggage storage, or a meal plan change. These requests automatically appear in your Guest Requests queue in the Operations area, ready for you to approve or decline.
